Tacos

Description

Carne asada tacos are delicious flank steak, Mexican-inspired street tacos! Just load up your tortilla with juicy steak bites, avocado, onion, cotija cheese, and fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ingredients to your liking.
  2. Just grab a tortilla and slather it with some avocado.
  3. Then, add a good helping of chopped carne asada, a sprinkle of cotija or queso fresco cheese, chopped onion and fresh cilantro.
  4. Squeeze some fresh lime on top.
